About Jenni

I am currently a student at Hope College, graduating in spring of 2017. I am looking into Biology, geology, the environmental sciences, studio art and writing as my potential majors.
Beginning in the fall of 2014, I have been a part of the Hope College Mellon Scholars. This is a group specializing in the digital humanities, research, and collaboration.
Honors I have received at Hope since 2013 include the Dean’s list, Distinguished Artist Award, and the Alumni Honors Scholarship.
My other interests include music, outdoor activities, traveling, and art.
